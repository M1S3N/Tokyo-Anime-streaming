ONE PIECE

Monkey. D. Luffy refuses to let anyone or anything stand in the way of his quest to become the king of all pirates. With a course charted for the treacherous waters of the Grand Line and beyond, this is one captain who'll never give up until he's claimed the greatest treasure on Earth: the Legendary One Piece!

NARUTO

Naruto Uzumaki is a determined ninja with big dreams. Despite being an outcast in his village and carrying the burden of a powerful demon sealed within him, Naruto is determined to become the greatest ninja and earn the respect of his peers. With his unwavering resolve and a spirit as fiery as his signature jutsu, Naruto embarks on a journey to become the strongest ninja of all and fulfill his dream of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Dub/Sub Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Shonen

Attack On Titan

In a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction, the remnants of civilization live within massive walled cities to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. Eren Yeager, along with his friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert, witness the horror of the Titans firsthand when their hometown is destroyed and their loved ones are killed.

Dub/Sub Action, Fantasy, Horror, Drama, Shonen

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

In the world of Demon Slayer, Tanjiro Kamado is a young and compassionate boy whose life is forever changed when his family is slaughtered by demons, leaving only his sister Nezuko, who has been transformed into a demon herself. Determined to avenge his family and find a way to cure Nezuko, Tanjiro joins the ranks of the Demon Slayer Corps.

Dub/Sub Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Supernatural, Shonen

Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori, a high school student with remarkable physical abilities, becomes involved in the world of Jujutsu after he swallows a cursed object containing the finger of the powerful demon, Sukuna. Determined to protect others, Yuji joins Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College to become a Jujutsu sorcerer.

Dub/Sub Action, Supernatural, Fantasy, Shonen, Horror
